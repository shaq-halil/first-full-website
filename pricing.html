HISTORY
MAQ powerwash is a young and indigenous company established on 23rd June, 2022. It focuses mainly on innovation to solve unresolved problems in the cleaning industry. Not forgetting the wide range of authentic products used in our daily operations. We are located in Tarkwa which happens to be the home of most mining companies in Ghana, as a result of that, MAQ powerwash is highly inclined in industrial laundry and cleaning
PRICING SYSTEM
At MAQ powerwash, we believe cleanliness shouldn't come at a premium. That's why we offer competitive pricing that fits your budget. We ensure you know exactly what you're paying for, with no hidden fees.We provide top-quality laundry services without breaking the bank, ensuring you get the best value for your hard-earned money. We recieve payment via a wide range of money transfer services from which you can choose from the one that best suits you.
HOW OUR SERVICES ARE
We handle your clothes with love, from start to finish. Our careful handling and attention to detail ensure your clothes look their best. clothes are recieved,treated with specialized solutions and washed with industrial grade machines. Followed by drying, pressing and bagging according to customer preference. Our professional drivers will collect your dirty laundry and return it clean, fresh, and folded, saving you time and effort. Track your order in real-time and receive notifications when your laundry is on its way
